About Our Project


Hello! Welcome to our page. We are so glad you're here!

We are Abraca-data, the creators of this online recommender system for you. Our team saw the need to address a nation-wide issue with nutrition in the United States, and wanted to share this interactive tool to help you discover the benefits of nutritional food intake as our solution.

We like to keep things simple, intuitive, and easy to understand with data. We want to make this data available to as many people as possible so YOU have access and are in charge of taking the first step forward to living a healthy lifestyle.

Our journey begins here.
