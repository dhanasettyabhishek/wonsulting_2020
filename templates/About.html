About Our Project


Hello! Welcome to our page. We are so glad you're here!

We are Abraca-data, the creators of this online recommender system for you. Our team saw the need to address a nation-wide issue with nutrition in the United States, and wanted to share this interactive tool to help you discover the benefits of nutritional food intake as our solution.

We like to keep things simple, intuitive, and easy to understand with data. We want to make this data available to as many people as possible so YOU have access and are in charge of taking the first step forward to living a healthy lifestyle.

Our journey begins here.

Our Mission

Our mission at Abraca-data is to develop a hassle-free experience with exploring food intake options based on your meals of the day

         

Our Values

At our core, Abraca-data uses data to make informed, driven decisions that will improve our lifestyles. We operate on logic, inclusivity, and impact.

Our Promise

We offer an online tool that is simple, intuitive, and easy to understand for our audience to recognize the value to nutrition in their daily lives.

         

Our Culture

At Abraca-data, we live in a world full of data. We explore, analyze, and find hidden patterns in datasets together. We visualize and tackle on those data challenges to reach the outcome solution.
